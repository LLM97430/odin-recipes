Coffee

Description

Every moral person should start his day with a coffee.

And get an additional one every hour of a working day.

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Turn the coffee machine on;
  2. Push the "expresso" button. (Other buttons exist for decoration purpose only);
  3. Take the sugar back where it belongs, which is is certainly not into your coffee cup;
  4. Appreciate the strong taste of a delicious hot drink offered by the nature, George Clooney and your boss if you're lucky enough.
