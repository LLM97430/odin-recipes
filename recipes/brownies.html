Brownies

Description

"I'm only human, don't put your blame on me..."

Even if this recipe has a lot of chances to make you addictive and gain weight.

Ingredients

  • A necessary amount of chocolate;
  • Sugar, please. A lot of sugar;
  • Certainly some flour will be needed too;
  • Eggs (until it looks okay);
  • A vanilla ice cream scope (mandatory).

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven;
  2. Mix all the ingredients above (except the ice cream, keep it for later);
  3. Put the mix into the oven;
  4. Take the brownies out of the oven before it get burnt;
  5. Cut the brownies into cute large enough pieces;
  6. Put each piece of the brownies in a different plate;
  7. Put the ice cream besides the brownies;
  8. A song of ice and fire. Enjoy.
